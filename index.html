About Me

Discover my journey and passion for innovation and technology.

Entrepreneur | Innovator | Final-Year System Development Student | Founder & CEO | Aspiring AI Professor & Astrophysicist

Hi, I'm Elvis Matimo Monama — a passionate **entrepreneur** and **innovator**, currently completing my **final year in System Development** at *Boston City Campus*. As the **Founder & CEO of E.Digital Marketing Web Corp**, I leverage leadership and technical expertise to drive progress in the digital marketing space.

I am the visionary behind **Elvis Matimo Academy**, an initiative set to revolutionize education in South Africa through **accessible, engaging, and high-quality learning experiences**.

I have also launched **ElvisMatimoBlueprints.com**, a website showcasing my journey, projects, and educational initiatives, designed to inspire and connect with learners and professionals worldwide.

My aspirations extend beyond business. With a deep passion for **AI, machine learning, astrophysics, and engineering**, I aim to achieve the titles of **Professor, Philosopher, and Doctor**, contributing to science, education, and technology at the highest levels.

In my academic journey, I have actively worked as an **intern** on a **cognitive enhancement project** at Boston City Campus, where I programmed apps and Moodle plugins. I am also building a **2D cognitive enhancement game in Unity**, combining creativity and technology to advance cognitive skills.

Beyond technical projects, I am developing **unique Udemy courses** to share expertise in **technology, systems development**, and **entrepreneurship**. Additionally, I manage **online stores on Printify**, offering creative clothing and products to global audiences.

With certifications in **Cyber Threat Management, Generative AI, Microsoft Technologies**, and a **C# certificate** from FreeCodeCamp and Microsoft, I possess a strong technical foundation and unwavering commitment to **innovation, education, and personal growth**.
